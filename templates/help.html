Instructions to play the game after logging in

1. Use the W,A,S,D keys to navigate through the game.

2. Use your mouse to move the player in the game.

3. Use the space bar to shoot.

4. Use the q button to acccess the portals (other rooms).

5. Portals are free and don't require payment from your accumulated points.

6. Use the e button to select interactables.

7. To shoot, hover your mouse on the zombie and use left-click to shoot at it.

8. Move the mouse to rotate the player.

9. Interactables require payment with points..
